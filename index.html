About Me

I am an experienced technical writer who thrives on making products easy to navigate.

My approach to writing is to understand the target audience and use cases. My intention is to verify that documentation will solve the problem, now and in the future.

If products are difficult to document, they are difficult to learn and use. For this reason, I work with engineering teams during all phases of design and development. This process enables me to ensure documentation and products align.

Technical writing is my passion because I am always learning something new. This keeps me engaged and excited to tackle new problems each day.
